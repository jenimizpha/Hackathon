The Problem

Phones and EVs suffer from battery degradation due to generic, sub-optimal charging patterns. Testing new physical strategies takes months of slow, expensive R&D.

✨

The Solution

Battery-Life Lab automates R&D. Our AI agent designs charging experiments, runs them in a virtual simulator overnight, and uses Bayesian optimization to find the perfect charging curve.
